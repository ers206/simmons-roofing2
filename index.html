ABOUT US

To sum us up, we are as old school as you can get. We are not trying to prove we're cutting edge by trying every new gimmick that comes out. With over 50 years of experience, we have gained the knowledge and expertise to understand what works and what doesn't work. You won't see us hiring flashy, smooth-talking salesmen or driving around in fancy trucks. We are just roofers who know the trade and do the work. We will not always be the cheapest bid, but we are committed to making sure we're the best. Our customers are people focused on making sure the job is done right. Quality over quantity is our motto, and it will never change. We take pride in being a small family business that takes the time to do the little extras for the homeowner.

OUR SERVICES

Asphalt Shingles

We specialize in the installation and repair of asphalt shingle roofs. Our skilled team ensures durable, weather-resistant, and aesthetically appealing roofing solutions.

Tear Offs

We provide efficient and meticulous tear-off services, removing old or damaged roofing materials with precision. Our goal is to prepare a solid foundation for the installation of new roofing systems.

EPDM-Flat Roofs

EPDM is a durable synthetic rubber roofing membrane (ethylene propylene diene terpolymer) widely used in low-slope structures

Repairs

We offer reliable roof repair services to address any issues that may arise, such as leaks, damaged shingles, or structural problems.

New Construction

Our expertise extends to new construction projects, where we collaborate closely with builders and architects to deliver high-quality roofing solutions. We ensure reliable and visually appealing roofs that complement the overall design.

Gutters Siding Etc

If the customer requires services such as gutters, siding, and related exterior projects, we take pride in connecting them with reliable professionals in those specific fields. We never seek any financial gain from these referrals.
